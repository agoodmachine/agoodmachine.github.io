_To access Capital's typography settings, select **Typography** from the **General settings** tab of your theme settings._

In Capital's typography settings, you can choose a distinct font face for base text and for header text. This affects your text store-wide, except on your shop's checkout pages. To customize the fonts on your checkout pages, see the [Checkout](../checkout) settings page.

Here is a list of Capital's available fonts:
